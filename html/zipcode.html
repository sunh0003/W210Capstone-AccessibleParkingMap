ACCESSIBILITY RATING - By zip code.

Audience: While our app is designed for individuals with disabilities using an assistive aid for mobility who need to find accessible parking easily, this high level visualization allows advocates and organizations to target accessibility interventions.

Handicap Parking Density: In the below map, the number of street-side handicap parking spots identified by our model are plotted per zipcode in Denver. While there is a high density of handicap parking located in the central and Downtown areas of Denver, this view of the data also hilights areas where few signs were found, meaning the availability of appropriate accessible parking should be assessed further.
